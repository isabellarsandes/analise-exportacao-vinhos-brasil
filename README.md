# Brazil Wine Export Analytics
Business analytics project exploring Brazilian wine exports, market drivers, and strategic growth insights.

---

## 📌 Project Context

This project simulates the role of a Data Analytics Expert in a Brazilian company that exports wine worldwide. The objective is to build analytical reports for investors and stakeholders, highlighting export performance and identifying external factors that may influence international sales.

---

## 🎯 Objective
Analyze the last 15 years of Brazilian wine export data in order to:

- Identify main destination countries
- Measure export volume (liters) and total revenue (USD)
- Understand export trends over time
- Evaluate external factors impacting exports:
  - Climate data
  - Demographic data
  - Economic indicators
  - Wine ratings
- Provide strategic insights and future growth opportunities

---

## 📊 Data Sources

- Brazilian wine export data (Embrapa - Vitibrasil)
- External datasets (economic, demographic, climate, and wine evaluation data)

---

## 🛠 Methodology

1. Data collection and integration
2. Data cleaning and transformation
3. Exploratory data analysis (EDA)
4. Correlation analysis with external factors
5. Trend analysis and forecasting
6. Strategic recommendations for export growth

---

## 📈 Key Business Questions

- Which countries import the highest volume of Brazilian wine?
- How have exports evolved over the last 15 years?
- Are there economic or demographic patterns influencing exports?
- What markets present future growth potential?

---

## 🚀 Expected Outcomes

- Executive-level visual dashboards
- Clear export performance insights
- Identification of high-potential markets
- Data-driven strategic recommendations
